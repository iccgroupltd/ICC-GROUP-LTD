When you've been involved in a non-fault accident, the last thing you need is to be left without transport. At ICC Taxi Claims, we provide reliable credit hire vehicles so you can carry on with your daily life while your vehicle is off the road.

## What Is Credit Hire?

Credit hire is a service that provides you with a replacement vehicle after a non-fault accident. The cost of the hire is recovered directly from the at-fault party's insurer — **you pay nothing**.

#### Our credit hire service includes:

- **Replacement vehicles** delivered to your door within 24 hours
- **Like-for-like replacement** — if you drive a taxi, we provide a taxi
- **No upfront costs** — we recover all fees from the at-fault insurer
- **Flexible hire periods** — we keep you mobile for as long as needed
- **Dedicated claims handler** — personal support throughout the process

## Who Is Eligible?

If you were involved in a road traffic accident that was **not your fault**, you are entitled to a credit hire vehicle. This applies to:

- Private drivers
- Taxi and PHV licence holders
- Fleet operators
- Business users

We handle the entire process on your behalf — from arranging the vehicle to recovering costs from the third-party insurer.
