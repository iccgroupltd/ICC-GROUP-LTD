After a non-fault accident, getting your vehicle repaired quickly and to the highest standard is essential. ICC Taxi Claims manages the entire repair process on your behalf, ensuring quality workmanship and a smooth claims process.

## Managed Repair Service

We take the stress out of vehicle repairs by managing the whole process from start to finish. The cost of repairs is recovered directly from the at-fault party's insurer.

### Our vehicle repair service includes:

- **Vehicle assessment** and damage inspection
- **Approved repair network** — vetted garages with guaranteed quality
- **Original manufacturer parts** used wherever possible
- **Collection and delivery** of your vehicle
- **Regular progress updates** so you're always informed
- **Lifetime repair guarantee** on all work carried out

## The Repair Process

1. **Report your accident** — contact us and we'll start your claim immediately
2. **Vehicle inspection** — we arrange a convenient time to assess the damage
3. **Repair authorisation** — we deal with the at-fault insurer directly
4. **Quality repairs** — your vehicle is repaired to the highest standard
5. **Vehicle return** — we deliver your vehicle back to you, fully repaired

## Taxi & Private Hire Vehicles

We specialise in taxi and private hire vehicle repairs. We understand that time off the road means lost income, so we prioritise fast turnaround times and can provide a replacement vehicle while yours is in the garage.
