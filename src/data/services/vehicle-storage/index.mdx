If your vehicle has been damaged in a non-fault accident and is not roadworthy, it needs to be stored safely while repairs are arranged. ICC Taxi Claims provides secure vehicle storage as part of our complete accident management service.

## Secure Storage Solutions

Our vehicle storage facilities offer peace of mind when your vehicle cannot be driven following an accident. All storage costs are recovered from the at-fault party's insurer.

#### Our vehicle storage service includes:

- **Secure, CCTV-monitored facilities** — your vehicle is kept safe 24/7
- **Indoor and outdoor storage options** — depending on your vehicle's needs
- **Fully insured premises** — comprehensive cover while in storage
- **Collection service** — we can collect your vehicle from any location
- **Ongoing condition reports** — documented evidence of your vehicle's state

## When Do You Need Vehicle Storage?

Vehicle storage is typically required when:

- Your vehicle is **not roadworthy** after an accident
- The vehicle is awaiting **repair authorisation** from an insurer
- There is a **write-off dispute** and the vehicle must be preserved for inspection
- You need to **preserve evidence** for a personal injury claim

Vehicle storage is just one part of our comprehensive accident management package. Combined with our credit hire and repair services, we ensure that from the moment of your accident to the return of your vehicle, everything is handled professionally from start to finish.
